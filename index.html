INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Information Technology is important in business, as it helps in cost reduction, better communication, improvement of financial management, improvement of public relations and time saving. IT is a vital requirement for success in the modern world of business.

PROGRAMMING

Programming is the process of designing, writing, testing, and maintaining computer programs. It involves the use of programming languages such as Python, Java, C++, and many others, to instruct computers to perform specific tasks or operations.

WEB-DEVELOPMENT

Web development involves designing, creating, and maintaining websites and web applications. It encompasses several aspects, including web design, front-end development, back-end development, and web server configuration.
